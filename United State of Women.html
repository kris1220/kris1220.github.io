Deeply rooted in principles of charity and eco-consciousness, House of Marley makes premium headphones and speakers that not only embody these tenets, but are also well-designed and easy to use. A challenger brand with a legacy to love up to, House of Marley has a strong vision and product catalog, but their previous site design didn't do the brand justice. Facing steep competition in a market oversaturated with slick, celeb-backed audio gear, the brand looked to DOMANI to increase conversions through a complete overhaul of their e-commerce site both here in the US and in markets abroad.
